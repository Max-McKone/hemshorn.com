üí≠ Questions for Reflection and Discussion

Questions that can lead to deeper insights:

Personal Development

  • What makes a fulfilled life for me?
  • Where do I stand in my own way?
  • What would I do if I knew I couldn't fail?
  • Which values are truly important to me?

Leadership

  • What kind of leader do I want to be?
  • How do I create an environment where others can grow?
  • Where do I confuse control with leadership?
  • What do my employees really need from me?

Change

  • What is the first small step I can take today?
  • What fears are holding me back?
  • What have I learned from past changes?
  • Who can support me on this path?

Collaboration

  • What does good collaboration mean to me?
  • Where can I give more trust?
  • How do I deal constructively with conflicts?
  • What can I contribute to make our team more successful?
