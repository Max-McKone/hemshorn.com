Coaching

Personal development in professional and private life.

You discover yourself, uncover what lies within you, expand your perspective, build competencies and capacities, define your values, and expand your scope of action.

Coaching Hamburg

You have big plans for the future, dream of an exemplary career and a fulfilled and happy private life. Often, due to past experiences, you stand in your own way. This is actually a completely human trait.

It only becomes problematic when you don't know your own filters and therefore can't work on yourself, when inner obstacles can't be recognized and cleared.

With coaching in Hamburg, I can help you gain a clear and new perspective. Questions like "Who am I?", "What do I want to achieve in life?" and "What talents and potentials lie hidden?" are illuminated. You adopt a different perspective, expand your scope of action, and the new perspectives fill you with new energy.

Learning to Think Differently with Coaching in Hamburg

Perhaps there are more pleasant things than holding up a mirror to yourself, but it is absolutely necessary for the self-determined development of your personality. Whether the glass is half empty or half full, you decide. Think outside the box!

In the context of coaching in Hamburg, I show you ways to say goodbye to patterns that repeatedly lead you off course in certain situations. During coaching in Hamburg, I convey essential building blocks for communication, empathy, and emotional intelligence, which are at least as important for sustainable success and satisfaction as competence and assertiveness.

Often, much more can be achieved through appreciation of a person than with reproaches and excessive severity, even towards oneself.

Explore the Unsuspected Talents and Strengths of Your Employees

Are you the head of a company and want to tap into the full potential of your employees and optimize the working atmosphere? Then you could benefit enormously from coaching in Hamburg. In a preliminary meeting, I get to know your company vision, challenges, and development goals. In structured person-centered coaching, I then work with your employees to develop the building blocks for high motivation and identification to achieve your company's goals.

I am also happy to provide consulting support in personnel selection and filling important positions, and together with you, we find out which employee or external applicant has the necessary know-how and emotional strength.

Here I work with proven (Bochum Inventory for Job-Related Personality Description - BIP) and innovative systems (Pro Human Resources - PHR) to create a picture of suitability that is much more comprehensive than application documents and interviews, giving you more certainty in personnel selection.

Together we remove the obstacles that make goal-oriented action difficult and bring you alternative approaches.
