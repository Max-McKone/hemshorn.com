Profile

Values

"It is what it is!"

Acceptance, openness and gratitude are, in my opinion, essential elements for a good life. Finding the way there is always a challenge. But increasingly, obstacles become signposts.

Making this potential visible is a concern of mine in my work. I have no formula for success, satisfaction or even happiness, but I have a large toolbox full of tools, experiences, methods and models that will advance you.

What is the goal? There are interim goals and often we think everything will be better when we have achieved them, only to find that a new goal immediately emerges. Nevertheless, we can develop skills that strengthen us across topics and indeed make everything better!

Everything between birth and death is a self-writing story in which we play, or should play, the leading role. We can only arrive at ourselves and fill our role in harmony with our values and wishes.

This attitude is also reflected in my work. It is about steering development from the core. To illuminate this, to accompany people, brands and companies in a development process is a task that fulfills me, inspires me and always inspires me anew.

Coaching, training and moderation are interactive processes at eye level. Every encounter, every process is new and exciting and I experience it as a gift.

My expertise, my experiences, my attention, empathy and strength are fully at your disposal in our joint work. The expertise for the process, for building blocks, methods and models lies with me. The answers, solutions and individual development steps lie within you. Together we create clarity, define development directions and bring about sustainable change.

At this point I would like to thank all my teachers, for all encounters and experiences and for the trust that is placed in me.

Nicola Hemshorn
