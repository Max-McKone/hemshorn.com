Training

Building competencies and capacities

Interactive Training and Workshops

In my training and workshops, I combine sound knowledge with practical application. Together we develop competencies that you can use directly in your professional everyday life.

My Training Topics

Leadership Competence

Develop your own leadership style. Learn to inspire and motivate teams. Improve your communication and decision-making.

Communication

Clear, appreciative communication in all situations. Conduct constructive conflict conversations. Give and receive feedback.

Team Development

Strengthen collaboration in your team. Develop common goals and values. Use diversity as a resource.

Resilience & Stress Management

Build mental strength. Develop strategies for dealing with stress. Find balance between performance and regeneration.

My Approach

My trainings are designed to be interactive and practical. I work with proven methods from systemic work, positive psychology, and modern leadership concepts. Each training is tailored to your specific needs and challenges.

Through a balanced mix of theoretical input, practical exercises, reflection, and exchange, we create sustainable learning success.

  • Multi-day intensive trainings
  • Modular training series
  • In-house trainings
  • Open seminars
