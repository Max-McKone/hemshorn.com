Strategic Development for Sustainable Impact

Successful companies invest in their most important resource: their people. As a partner for HR development and organizational culture, I support you in developing a vibrant, appreciative corporate culture and accompanying your employees in their personal and professional development.

HR Development

Aligning individual development with organizational goals - that's the core of modern HR development. I support you with:

  • Leadership Development: Building and strengthening leadership competencies at all levels
  • Talent Development: Identifying and specifically promoting potential
  • Career Planning: Designing individual development paths
  • Onboarding Programs: Successfully integrating new employees
  • Personnel Selection: Finding the right people for your company

Organizational Culture

Culture is not what's written on the website, but what is lived daily. I support you in developing a culture that fits your vision and is supported by your employees:

  • Culture Analysis: Where do you stand today? What defines your culture?
  • Values Development: Defining and bringing common values to life
  • Cultural Change: Supporting transformation processes
  • Leadership Culture: Developing and strengthening leadership culture
  • Feedback Culture: Establishing constructive exchange

My Approach

I work holistically and systemically. This means: I don't just look at individual measures, but at the entire system with its interactions. My work is based on:

Analysis & Diagnosis

Thoroughly understanding the current situation, challenges, and potentials.

Co-Creation

Developing tailored solutions together with you and your employees.

Implementation

Practical implementation with clear structures and responsibilities.

Sustainability

Evaluation and continuous adjustment for long-term impact.

Diagnostics & Assessment

For well-founded personnel selection and development, I work with proven and innovative instruments:

  • BIP (Bochum Inventory for Job-Related Personality Description): Scientifically based method for assessing professionally relevant personality traits
  • PHR (Pro Human Resources): Innovative systems for potential analysis and competency development
  • 360-Degree Feedback: Comprehensive feedback from different perspectives
  • Development Conversations: Structured conversations for position determination and goal development

These instruments give you more certainty in personnel selection and development and enable an objective, comprehensive assessment that goes far beyond application documents and interviews.
